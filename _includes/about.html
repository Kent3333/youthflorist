About

Youth Florist since 2018, always focus on provide high quality flowers and excellent services for our customers. Our flower shop was founded by a young floral designer who's creative and passionate, with years of experience in floral art. In just a few years, we have earned the trust and good reviews from many customers, and become one of the well-known flower shops in Johor Bahru.

We are committed to creating beautiful, unique, trendy, and thoughtful floral works, to provide our customers with a satisfying shopping experience. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, proposal or holiday gift, we can tailor the most suitable bouquet for you. We hope to add more colors and beautiful memories to your life through our floral art.
